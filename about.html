What is 'Greetings From Space'?

A long time ago (2011), in a galaxy far, far away (somewhere in Canada)...

...there was a web comic called "Greetings From Space". Updates were inconsistent, the jokes were hit or miss, and the comic itself garnered little attention outside of the artist's small circle of friends and family. Regardless, the artist enjoyed the creative outlet it offered and carried on... until he didn't, because jokes are hard and don't write themselves.

Attempts were made to revive the project over the years, but laziness would inevitably prevail, and "Greetings From Space" would time and again recede into the background of life. Now, in 2019, an archive has been constructed; a new home and final resting place on the web for an idea that was probably never worth having in the first place.

What does the future hold for Greetings From Space? Is it finally dead? No, not really. I will be posting comics sporadically as I have the time and as inspiration strikes. Stay tuned!

'Greetings From Space' Creator, Andrew James Clanahan
