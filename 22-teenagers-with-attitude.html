#22 Teenagers With AttitudeMarch 28, 2017

Commentary

"This comic was originally created in March of 2017, but remained unpublished until August of 2019. While it's not the worst comic I've ever made, I opted to publish this Power Ranger-themed comic instead, created over the same few days. I thought two Power Rangers comics in a row would have been a bit overkill, so chose to hold this one back. I'm posting it now for the sake of posterity, as to me it has always been a part of the main collection."
