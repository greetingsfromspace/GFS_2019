We Have Liftoff!

Welcome to the brand new Greetings From Space website!
Greetings From Space #1, January 11 2011

Hard to believe it's been almost nine years since the original launch of this comic. Although I've neglected it over the years, I have never forgotten about it, and always planned to do something more with it. I thought it was due time to give these comics their own website again (designed and coded by yours truly!)

What are my plans for this site? Well, first and foremost, to archive what I've already done in a way that does the comics justice. You'll find, for instance, my personal commentary on each and every archive page, which give a little more insight into the thinking behind each comic.

As for the future, I do intend to keep the comic alive. In fact, there's a new comic on the front page right now! I won't be keeping a regular schedule, but I will update the site whenever inspiration strikes, and hopefully that will be more often than not. Check back here or on Facebook for updates!

The old site will remain online for the time being as a backup.
